welcome to my poems world

Ki ab jo tum baag e chaman hamara chhod rahe ho, Humne kiya kya tumhare liye Y keh chuke ho To tum khizaan e naadina ho e dost Jisne dekhi ho pasti Tum wo urooj nahi e dost Ek arse k talluq ke baad Ye sb sehna padega Tum gair ho Na chahkar bhi y kehna padega

I looked for signs in stars and skies, In waves, in trees, in passerby. I thought He’d speak in something loud, A flash of light, a voice, a parting cloud. But what I found was something small— A quiet feeling, that was all. I found quiet peace— A calm that made my questions cease. No grand reply, no sudden sign, no big reveal Just a small whisper: “I’ve always been the One.”
